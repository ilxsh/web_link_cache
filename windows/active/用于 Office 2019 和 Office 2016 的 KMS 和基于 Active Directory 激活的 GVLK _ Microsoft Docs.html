用于 Office 2019 和 Office 2016 的 KMS 和基于 Active Directory 激活的 GVLKGVLKs for KMS and Active Directory-based activation of Office 2019 and Office 2016

  • 2019/10/18

本文内容

适用于： Office 2019 和 Office 2016（包括 Project 和 Visio）的批量许可版本Applies to: Volume licensed versions of Office 2019 and Office 2016, including Project and Visio

默认情况下，批量许可版本的 Office 2019 和 Office 2016 将使用通用批量许可证密钥 (GVLK) 安装。By default, volume licensed versions of Office 2019 and Office 2016 are installed with a Generic Volume License Key (GVLK). GVLK 允许 Office 自动针对 KMS 主计算机或 Active Directory 基础架构进行发现和激活。The GVLK enables Office to automatically discover and activate against your KMS host computer or Active Directory infrastructure.

重要

这些许可证密钥无法用于激活你的 Office 个人副本。These license keys can't be used to activate your personal copy of Office. 有关 Office 个人副本产品密钥的信息，请参阅对 Office 使用产品密钥。For information about product keys for a personal copy of Office, see Using product keys with Office.
